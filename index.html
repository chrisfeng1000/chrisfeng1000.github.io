Chris Feng

Hi there! I am a Research Assistant at the Machine Learning Department and the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. I am advised by Prof. Barnabas Poczos and Prof. Nicholas M. Boffi. My research interests focus on AI for Science (AI4Bio), generative models, flow models, diffusion models, math-inspired deep learning, and interpretable deep learning.

I obtained my master's degree from Carnegie Mellon University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zackory Erickson to work on signal processing research.

Email is the best way to reach me and please feel free to send me an email to discuss research! I try to read all my emails carefully, but don't hesitate to send another one if you don't receive my reply after one week!
